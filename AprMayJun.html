KAWASAN FALLS

One of the best outdoor adventure tours in the Philippines is the Kawasan Falls tour in Cebu. This waterfall is popular for its turquoise-colored water that you can dive into from the top of the falls. The falls was originally known as “Mantayupan Falls”, named after the barangay it was located in. In the early 1980s, the municipality of Badian decided to develop the area into a tourist spot.
